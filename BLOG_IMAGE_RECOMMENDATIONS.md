## Image Specifications

### Technical Requirements:
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1200x800 pixels (16:9 ratio preferred)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File Size:** Under 500KB for web optimization
- **Quality:** High quality, professional photography

### Style Guidelines:
- **Lighting:** Natural, well-lit images
- **Composition:** Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
- **Colors:** Vibrant but natural colors
- **Subjects:** Diverse representation of riders (age, gender, ethnicity)
- **Setting:** Realistic, relatable environments

### Image Sources:
- **Stock Photo Services:** Unsplash, Pexels, Shutterstock, iStock
- **Custom Photography:** Professional bike photography
- **User-Generated Content:** Customer photos (with permission)
