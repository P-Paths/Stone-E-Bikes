# The Electric Bike Revolution

Electric bikes (e-bikes) are revolutionizing urban transportation, making cycling accessible to more people than ever before. Whether you're dealing with hills, longer distances, or just want a little extra help, e-bikes open up new possibilities.

## What Makes E-Bikes Special?

### Motor Assistance
E-bikes provide pedal assistance, meaning:
- The motor only engages when you pedal
- You can adjust the level of assistance
- You still get exercise, just with less effort

### Extended Range
With motor assistance, you can:
- Tackle longer commutes without arriving sweaty
- Conquer hills that might otherwise be challenging
- Carry heavier loads more easily

## Types of E-Bikes

### 1. City/Commuter E-Bikes
- Comfortable upright position
- Integrated lights and fenders
- Often include racks for carrying items

### 2. Mountain E-Bikes
- Rugged construction for off-road use
- Powerful motors for steep climbs
- Full suspension options available

### 3. Folding E-Bikes
- Compact for storage and transport
- Perfect for multimodal commuting
- Lighter weight options available

## Key Features to Consider

### Battery Life
- Range typically 20-80 miles per charge
- Consider your daily needs
- Removable batteries are convenient for charging

### Motor Power
- 250W motors (legal limit in many areas)
- 500W+ for more power (check local regulations)
- Mid-drive vs. hub motors have different characteristics

### Price Range
E-bikes span a wide price range:
- **Entry-level**: $800-1500
- **Mid-range**: $1500-3000  
- **Premium**: $3000+
